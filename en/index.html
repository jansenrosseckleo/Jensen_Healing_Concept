Vagus nerve stimulation for deep relaxation. A gentle wellness treatment that aims to activate the vagus nerve — one of the most important elements of our nervous system for rest and regeneration.

What is the Vagus Nerve?

The vagus nerve is the longest cranial nerve and connects the brain with vital organs throughout the body. It plays a central role in our inner balance and our ability to reach a state of deep relaxation.

Gentle stimulation of this nerve is a modern wellness method that aims to promote the natural balance of the nervous system and support a feeling of calm and equilibrium.
